TABLE 2:
#	Stage	Key Actions & System Behaviour	Control
1	Purchase Requisition (PR)	Requester raises need (item / asset / service) with quantity, specification, cost centre, budget code and justification. AI suggests catalogue items and checks budget availability.	Auto-assist
2	PR Approval	Routed through the multi-level approval matrix by value, category and cost centre. Electronic signature captured; auto-escalation on SLA breach.	Approval
3	Sourcing / RFQ	For non-contracted items, RFQ issued to shortlisted vendors via the Vendor Portal. Quotes collected; AI performs comparative scoring.	Auto-assist
4	Vendor & Quote Selection	Best-value vendor selected (rule- or AI-recommended). Justification recorded for non-lowest selection.	Approval
5	Purchase Order (PO)	PO auto-generated from approved PR and selected quote; terms, tax and delivery schedule applied.	Auto
6	PO Approval & Dispatch	PO approved per DOA, then dispatched to vendor by email/portal. Amendments tracked with version history.	Approval
7	Order Acknowledgement	Vendor acknowledges PO and confirms delivery date via the portal; ASN optionally submitted.	Auto
8	Goods Receipt (GRN)	On delivery, stores scan barcodes against the PO; GRN auto-created with quantity, short/excess and batch/serial capture.	Auto
9	Quality Inspection (QC)	Inspection with accept / reject / quarantine outcome. For regulated items, release follows QC approval; assets are tagged on acceptance.	Approval
10	Invoice Capture & 3-Way Match	Vendor invoice received; AI extracts data and matches PO – GRN – Invoice within tolerance. Clean matches auto-approve; exceptions are flagged.	Auto
11	AP & Payment Approval	Matched invoice posted to accounts payable with GL coding and GST validation; payment approved per DOA.	Approval
12	Payment & Reconciliation	Payment executed (NEFT/RTGS/UPI/bank file); remittance advice issued; vendor ledger updated; PO closed and transaction reconciled.	Auto

TABLE 6:
Step	Action	System Behaviour
1	Generate & Print	System generates unique barcode/QR labels for assets, items, bins and documents.
2	Scan	Android app or handheld scanner captures the code, working online or offline at the point of activity.
3	Validate & Post	Cloud platform validates the transaction against master data and business rules, then posts it in real time.
4	Decide (AI/Rules)	Automation engine evaluates the transaction, auto-completes routine cases and routes exceptions to people.
5	Synchronise	Asset, WMS and Inventory/Purchase records update simultaneously — a single source of truth.
6	Visualise	Web and mobile dashboards reflect the updated position immediately for management and operational users.
